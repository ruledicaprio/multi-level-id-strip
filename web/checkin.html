Demo by mlis — photograph a guest's passport or ID card and the registration form fills itself. Deterministic ICAO 9303 validation, not an AI guess.

Green-bordered fields were read from the document and verified by its printed check digits — review, add the stay dates, then paste into your registration system (eVisitor, eTurista, …). This demo does not submit anywhere by itself.

A ✔ verdict means every ICAO 9303 check digit verifies — the read provably matches the printed machine-readable zone. It does not verify document authenticity. Camera tips: fill the frame with the data page, avoid glare on the MRZ band.
